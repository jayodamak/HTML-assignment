    Choose your visit duration



  • The Hikkaduwa turtle hatchery operates all seven days of the week from 07:00 AM to 6:00 PM.



  • The entire day has been divided into 1-hour based time slots, and you can select your preferred duration from the drop-down menu below:



    • Peak Hours:

  • Please note that every day from 10:00 AM to 1:00 PM and from 3:00 PM to 6:00 PM will be considered as Peak hours.



    • Booking Information:

  • You can select a single one-hour time slot or choose multiple consecutive one-hour time slots from the drop-down menu.



  • Infants (both SL and Foreigner) under 4 years of age have free entrance.



    • Price:
